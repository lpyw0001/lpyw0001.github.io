Concluding Remarks

In summary, this project analyses Mastercard's actions towards addressing the sustainable development goals. The goals analysed include zero hunger, gender equality, decent work and economic growth, sustainable cities and communities and partnerships. However, the primary goal focused on was mitigation strategies towards climate change. From the analysis performed, it was found Mastercards vision to create a sustainable digital economy can complement the United Nation's goals. Mastercard have several effective initiatives in place working towards the accomplishment of goal 13, however to stay ahead of future regulatory changes, mitigate the consequences of inaction against climate chant and benefit the company's bottom-line long-term, Mastercard should supplement these. A simple strategy is to be transparent about emissions reduction opposed to the purchase of credits. To complement this strategy Mastercard should provide capital investments in carbon removal technology, as this strategy is less risky than the proposed political pressure ultimatum, but can still achieve widespread positive impacts. This strategy also requires less business deviation than incentivisation towards a shared economy.
